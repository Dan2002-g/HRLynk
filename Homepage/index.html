Annual Individual Development Plan (AIDP)

The Annual Individual Development Plan (AIDP) is a competency-based personal action plan, jointly agreed by the employee and the supervisor. An AIDP also identifies the training, and other developmental experiences needed to achieve those goals, for the benefit of the individual and organization, within a specified time. It is a tool for objective and competency-based learning and development management system for MSU-IIT which aims to identify priority competency development targets/needs of officials and employees; provide Learning and Development interventions that are appropriate to bridge the competency gaps, enhance competencies, improve job performance, increase productivity and develop the MSU-IIT workforce.
